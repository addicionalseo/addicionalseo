\# Addicionalseo Premium UI System



You are responsible for making Addicionalseo feel visually coherent across the whole website.



\## Goal



Create a premium design system that feels custom, elegant and consistent.



\## Global UI rules



\- Typography must feel intentional.

\- Spacing must feel generous but not empty.

\- Buttons must feel premium and clickable.

\- Cards must not look like default template cards.

\- Section backgrounds must have rhythm and contrast.

\- Gradients must be subtle and controlled.

\- Shadows must be refined.

\- Borders must be elegant.

\- Rounded corners must be consistent.

\- Mobile layout must feel designed, not just stacked.



\## Buttons



Buttons should:

\- have clear hierarchy,

\- use strong labels,

\- feel tactile,

\- include elegant hover states,

\- avoid generic blue button styling,

\- be consistent across the website.



Primary CTA examples:

\- Solicitar una auditoría

\- Quiero mejorar mi web

\- Hablemos de tu proyecto

\- Ver cómo podemos ayudarte



Avoid weak CTAs:

\- Saber más

\- Enviar

\- Clic aquí

\- Más información



\## Cards



Cards must have a purpose.



Avoid:

\- generic icon + title + text cards,

\- repeated equal-height blocks with no soul,

\- weak icons,

\- excessive shadows,

\- boring white rectangles.



Improve cards with:

\- custom headers,

\- number systems,

\- side labels,

\- hover depth,

\- editorial layout,

\- small visual indicators,

\- stronger copy,

\- varied rhythm.



\## Section rhythm



A premium page should alternate:

\- impact,

\- explanation,

\- proof,

\- process,

\- service detail,

\- trust,

\- CTA.



Do not make every section look equally important.



\## Visual consistency



Before finishing, check:

\- headings,

\- button styles,

\- card styles,

\- spacing,

\- colors,

\- border radius,

\- responsive breakpoints,

\- background transitions,

\- CTA consistency.



\## Mobile quality



Mobile must not be an afterthought.



Check:

\- no cramped text,

\- no oversized hero,

\- no broken visual layers,

\- no tiny buttons,

\- no excessive vertical gaps,

\- no horizontal scroll,

\- no clipped animations.



\## Final standard



The UI must make Addicionalseo look expensive, credible and designed by professionals.
