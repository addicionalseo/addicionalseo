\# Addicionalseo Anti AI Template



Your job is to detect and remove anything that makes Addicionalseo look like an AI-generated website or a generic template.



\## Things that look AI-generated



Flag and improve:

\- repeated 3-card grids,

\- vague gradients,

\- meaningless abstract blobs,

\- generic icons,

\- excessive rounded cards,

\- identical spacing in every section,

\- predictable hero + cards + FAQ rhythm,

\- generic titles like “Impulsa tu negocio digital” without a sharp idea,

\- stock-style layout,

\- weak CTAs,

\- sections that could belong to any agency,

\- empty marketing phrases,

\- fake premium effects,

\- random decorative elements,

\- huge sections with little content,

\- perfectly symmetrical layouts that feel lifeless.



\## Replace generic design with human design



Instead of generic sections, create:

\- editorial layouts,

\- strong visual anchors,

\- asymmetric compositions,

\- custom service blocks,

\- storytelling sections,

\- scroll rhythm,

\- contrast between calm and impact,

\- intentional negative space,

\- small crafted details,

\- visual metaphors related to SEO, growth, strategy, architecture, search, design systems and digital visibility.



\## Human-made details



Add details that feel designed by a person:

\- subtle section dividers,

\- custom badges,

\- unique CTA modules,

\- sticky visual elements,

\- small hover states,

\- carefully styled lists,

\- unexpected layout breaks,

\- premium typography pairings,

\- rhythm changes,

\- hand-crafted microcopy,

\- meaningful visual hierarchy.



\## Content rules



Do not use filler copy. Every text block must:

\- say something concrete,

\- sell a clear benefit,

\- show expertise,

\- reduce doubt,

\- increase trust,

\- guide the user to contact.



\## Before finishing



Ask yourself:

1\. Could this page belong to 1,000 other agencies?

2\. Does it look like a Tailwind template?

3\. Does it feel too symmetrical?

4\. Are the cards generic?

5\. Are the icons meaningless?

6\. Does it have a memorable moment?



If yes, keep improving.



\## Final standard



The page must feel custom, alive and designed with human taste.
